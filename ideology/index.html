// Their Own Words — DSA National Platform 2021
What DSA Actually Believes
// Anti-Capitalism
DSA Rejects Capitalism — Entirely
This is not a fringe interpretation. It is the opening position of DSA's national platform, published on DSA.org and ratified by the national convention.
"We are socialists because we reject an international economic order sustained by private profit, alienated labor, brutal force, and the elevation of property rights over human rights."
DSA National Platform 2021 — Published on DSA.org — Ratified by National Convention
// Abolish Police
DSA Calls for Abolishing Police, Prisons, and ICE
DSA's official platform calls for abolishing ICE, defunding police, and closing prisons. This is not a moderate reform position — it is the elimination of federal immigration enforcement and the dismantling of the criminal justice system. Avila Chevalier: "All deportations are wrong" — including for convicted criminals. AOC voted NO on additional Capitol Police funding after January 6.
"We call for the abolition of ICE and all detention facilities, and the decriminalization of all immigration status offenses."
DSA National Platform 2021 — Immigration Section — DSA.org
// Housing Seizure
Mamdani's Plan: Strip Buildings from Landlords
Mamdani's NYC housing platform includes stripping rental buildings from landlords and transferring them to tenant ownership. DSA's national platform calls for universal rent control and social housing — government ownership of residential real estate. This is not rent stabilization. It is property seizure.
"Social housing — publicly owned, community controlled, permanently affordable — must replace the speculative housing market."
DSA National Platform 2021 — Housing Section · Mamdani Campaign Platform 2025
// Medicare for All
Eliminate Private Health Insurance
DSA's Medicare for All platform calls for the complete elimination of private health insurance in the United States. Not a public option. Not expanded Medicare eligibility. Total elimination of the private market covering 180 million Americans. All seven DSA members of Congress have co-sponsored Medicare for All legislation.
"DSA demands the elimination of private health insurance and the establishment of a single-payer system covering all residents."
DSA National Platform 2021 — Healthcare Section — DSA.org
// Green New Deal
Government Control of Energy Production
DSA's climate platform calls for government ownership of energy production — not regulation of private energy companies, but the direct replacement of the private energy sector with government-run utilities. The estimated cost of the DSA-backed Green New Deal is $93 trillion over ten years.
"Public ownership of energy production is not optional — it is the only path to a just and sustainable economy."
DSA Green New Deal Resolution 2019 — National Convention

The Track Record

Venezuela — DSA's Praised Model

DSA members praised Hugo Chavez's socialist government as recently as 2019. The result: 7.7 million refugees — the largest refugee crisis in the Western Hemisphere — from what was once the richest country in South America. Oil-rich Venezuela now has a 76% poverty rate under Maduro's socialist government. Food rationing is standard. Basic medicine is unavailable.
