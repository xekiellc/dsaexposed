Overview

Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez is the U.S. Representative for NY-14 and the founding figure of DSA's congressional presence. Her 2018 upset of Joe Crowley launched the DSA electoral strategy that culminated in the 2026 sweep. A DSA member, she co-founded Justice Democrats — the PAC that has deployed over $11.2M for DSA-aligned candidates. She endorsed Mayor Mamdani for NYC Mayor in 2025.

Their Own Words

It is not hyperbole to say that the United States has committed human rights abuses at our border.

Congressional Floor Speech — Comparing CBP detention to Nazi concentration camps

On the Record

  • Iron Dome vote: Voted NO on $1 billion in Iron Dome missile defense funding for Israel (Roll Call 309, September 23, 2021). One of only nine House members to vote against.
  • Hamas condemnation: Voted PRESENT on the resolution condemning Hamas's October 7 attack — effectively refusing to condemn.
  • BDS support: Publicly supports BDS movement. Refuses to call Hamas a terrorist organization when asked directly.
  • Mamdani endorsement: Endorsed Zohran Mamdani for NYC Mayor in the 2025 primary.
  • DSA member: Has been a DSA member since before her 2018 election and maintains membership.
