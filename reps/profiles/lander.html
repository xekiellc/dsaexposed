Overview

Brad Lander is the Democratic nominee for NY-10, covering Lower Manhattan and Brooklyn. Endorsed by Mayor Mamdani, he defeated 2-term incumbent Dan Goldman in the June 2026 primary. A former DSA member, Lander served as NYC Comptroller and ran as Mamdani's chosen candidate for the congressional seat. His victory party featured 'Free Free Palestine' chants.
