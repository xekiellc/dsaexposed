Overview

Claire Valdez is the Democratic nominee for NY-7, covering parts of Brooklyn and Queens. A DSA member, she defeated 16-term incumbent Nydia Velazquez in the June 2026 primary with $370K from Justice Democrats and $455K from American Priorities PAC. She is endorsed by Mayor Mamdani and is expected to win the general election in the overwhelmingly Democratic district.
