Overview

Ilhan Omar is the U.S. Representative for MN-05, covering Minneapolis and the Cedar-Riverside neighborhood. DSA-endorsed, she is one of the first two Muslim women elected to Congress. CAIR — named an unindicted co-conspirator in the Holy Land Foundation Hamas financing trial — bundled $180,000+ for her campaigns.
