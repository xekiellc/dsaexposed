Overview

Rashida Tlaib is the U.S. Representative for MI-12, covering Detroit and Dearborn. She is the first Palestinian-American woman elected to Congress. DSA-endorsed, she was censured by the U.S. House in November 2023 for promoting the Hamas rallying cry 'from the river to the sea.' CAIR — named an unindicted co-conspirator in the Holy Land Foundation Hamas financing trial — has bundled $140,000+ for her campaigns.
