// Standards
Our Operating Principles
01
Primary Sources Only
Every claim on this site links to a primary source — congressional records, FEC filings, court documents, verified video, official org publications. We do not cite secondary reporting as evidence.
02
No Inference — Only Fact
We do not speculate. We do not editorialize claims as fact. If a connection is documented, we publish it with its source. If it is not documented, it does not appear on this site.
03
Independent Operation
DSA Exposed accepts no advertising, no PAC funding, and no government grants. We are independently operated with no financial relationship with any political campaign, party, or advocacy organization.
04
Verification Before Publication
Nothing is published without independent verification. Tips submitted through this site are reviewed by our research team and cross-referenced with primary sources before any content is added.
05
Permanent Archive
Content on this site is archived permanently. Statements that are deleted, accounts that are suspended, and documents that disappear — we keep them. The record does not get scrubbed.
06
Free & Open Access
Everything on DSA Exposed is free to read, share, and reference. No paywall. No subscription. No account required. This information belongs to the American public.
