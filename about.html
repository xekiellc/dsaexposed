About & Methodology

DSAExposed.com is an independent watchdog operation documenting the Democratic Socialists of America — their words, funding, terrorism ties, communist ideology, and systematic infiltration of American government.

The mainstream media will not cover this fully. DSAExposed.com will.

What We Document

We document through four pillars — the same framework used across every profile, section, and filing on this site:

W

Words

Floor speeches, social media posts, convention resolutions, campaign statements, rap lyrics, interviews. Every statement is dated, sourced, and linked to its original context. We do not paraphrase when we can quote directly.

F

Funding

FEC filings, PAC disclosures, IRS Form 990 filings, court records. Who writes the checks, how much, and what the donor's documented ties are. All figures traced to public filings.

S

Support

Endorsements, co-appearances, shared platforms, event attendance. Who they march with, who they headline for, who they accept support from. Documented with dates and sources.

N

Networks

Organizational relationships, coalition memberships, shared infrastructure. The connections between DSA and terror-adjacent groups, Islamist organizations, and foreign-linked networks.

Our Six Operating Principles

01

Primary Sources Only

Every claim on this site links to a primary source — congressional records, FEC filings, court documents, verified video, official organizational publications. We do not cite secondary reporting as evidence of a fact. Secondary reporting may point us to a primary source; the primary source is what we cite.

02

No Inference — Only Fact

We do not speculate about motives, intentions, or unstated beliefs. If a connection is documented, we publish it with its source. If it is not documented, it does not appear on this site. We draw no conclusions that the primary record does not support.

03

Independent Operation

DSAExposed.com accepts no advertising, no PAC funding, and no government grants. We are independently operated with no financial relationship with any political campaign, party, advocacy organization, or foreign entity.

04

Verification Before Publication

Nothing is published without independent verification against a primary source. Tips submitted through this site are reviewed and cross-referenced before any content is added or updated.

05

Permanent Archive

Content on this site is archived permanently. Deleted statements, suspended accounts, removed documents — we keep them. The record does not get scrubbed. Where possible, we archive to the Internet Archive and document the original URL alongside the archive link.

06

Free & Open Access

Everything on DSAExposed.com is free to read, share, cite, and reference. No paywall. No subscription. No account required. This information belongs to the American public.

Corrections Policy

If a factual error exists on this site, we want to know about it. Contact us at info@dsaexposed.com with documentation of the error and the correct information. Verified corrections are published promptly and noted inline. We do not silently delete corrected content — we note what was changed and why.

What This Site Is Not

DSAExposed.com is not a hate site. Criticism of a political organization, an ideology, or an individual public official's documented public statements and votes is not hate speech — it is protected political speech under the First Amendment.

We document public figures' public statements, public votes, public funding, and public organizational affiliations. We do not publish private information, personal addresses, private communications, or anything obtained through unauthorized means.

We are not affiliated with the Republican Party, any conservative PAC, any foreign government, or any political campaign. We do not endorse candidates.

Legal Notice

All statements attributed to individuals are sourced and linked to their original context. This site constitutes protected speech under the First Amendment to the United States Constitution.
